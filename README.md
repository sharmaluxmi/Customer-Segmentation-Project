# Customer-Segmentation-Project
Flipkart Customer Segmentation Analysis
Project Overview
Customer Segmentation is one of the most important business analytics techniques used to classify customers into meaningful groups based on their purchasing behavior, spending patterns, and preferences.

This project analyzes Flipkart sales data and develops an interactive Power BI dashboard to identify customer segments, understand revenue-driving customers, and provide actionable business insights for targeted marketing and customer retention strategies.

The project combines Data Cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Customer Analytics, and Business Intelligence Visualization to support data-driven decision making.

Business Problem
E-commerce businesses serve thousands of customers with different buying habits and preferences. Applying the same marketing strategy to every customer often leads to lower conversion rates and customer dissatisfaction.

The goal of this project is to:

Identify different customer groups.
Analyze customer purchase behavior.
Understand category-wise and city-wise sales performance.
Study payment preferences.
Generate actionable business recommendations.
Improve customer retention and profitability.
Objectives
Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
Segment customers based on spending and purchase frequency.
Identify high-value customers.
Discover top-performing product categories.
Evaluate city-wise revenue contribution.
Study customer payment preferences.
Create an interactive Power BI dashboard.

Key Insights
High-value customers contribute the largest portion of revenue.
Electronics and Home categories generate significant sales.
Digital payment methods dominate transactions.
Metropolitan cities contribute the highest revenue.
Seasonal sales trends influence monthly revenue patterns.
Business Recommendations
Focus loyalty programs on high-value customers.
Personalize marketing campaigns for medium-value customers.
Create re-engagement campaigns for low-value customers.
Promote high-performing categories.
Expand inventory in top-performing cities.
Encourage digital payments through incentives.
